Our Mission

Zomi Center is a nonprofit community organization built by Zomi people for Zomi people. We exist to make sure no Zomi family carries the burden of burial costs, cultural isolation, or language loss alone.

We are not tied to any church, denomination, or political group. You can practice your faith freely — or not — and still belong here.

What We Do

Burial Support

Dignified funeral assistance so families can grieve without financial fear. No church membership required.

Community Care

Connect with Zomi people in your city. Find help with translation, legal aid, housing, and daily needs.

Language & Culture

Access the Zomi AI model, Bible datasets, educational materials, and cultural preservation projects.

Zomi AI

We built the world's first Zomi language AI — a Qwen 3B model fine-tuned on Tedim Bible texts, educational books, and conversational Zomi. It speaks proper Zomi, not mixed dialects.
